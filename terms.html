What 854 COWS Is

854 COWS is a simple text messaging service for Tillamook County, Oregon. You text us questions about local businesses, events, weather, or community info. We text back answers. That's the whole service.

How to Use It

Just text 541-854-2697 with any question about Tillamook.
Examples:

  • "What's for dinner?"
  • "Any live music tonight?"
  • "When is high tide tomorrow?"
  • "What events are this weekend?"

We'll text you back with an answer, then forget the conversation happened.

How to Opt In

You opt in by texting us. That's it.
You can find our number by:

  • Scanning QR codes around town
  • Visiting 854cows.com
  • Someone telling you about it
  • However you want

Your first text to us means you consent to get a response. We only respond - we never text you first.

Message Frequency and Costs

Message frequency: Only when you text us first. We don't send newsletters, promotions, or regular updates. Zero messages unless you start the conversation.
Message and data rates may apply according to your phone plan. We don't charge anything - your carrier might.

For Businesses

Listing your business is free. Adding unlimited updates like specials and hours is $25/month.
Text: "REGISTER [Your Business Name] [Category]" to 541-854-2697
Examples:

  • "REGISTER Tony's Pizza restaurant"
  • "REGISTER Tillamook Library service"

Subscribed businesses can add unlimited updates by texting from the same number:

  • "SPECIAL Large pizza $12 today only"
  • "HOURS Mon-Fri 9am-6pm"
