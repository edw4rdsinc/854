About 854 COWS

854 COWS is a community information service that provides local business information, events, weather, and other community updates for Tillamook County, Oregon via SMS text messaging.

How to Opt In

You can start receiving messages from 854 COWS in several ways:

  • Direct SMS: Text any question to 541-854-2697 (541-854-COWS). Your first message serves as your consent to receive responses.
  • QR Code/Website: Scan our QR codes or visit 854cows.com, then text us your first question.
  • Business Registration: Local businesses can text "REGISTER [Business Name] [Category]" to join our directory.
  • Word of Mouth: Others may tell you about the service - simply text us to start.

What Messages You'll Receive

When you text 854 COWS, you'll receive:

  • Response messages answering your specific questions about local businesses, events, weather, tide times, and community information
  • Business information including hours, contact details, and current specials
  • Event listings for community activities and entertainment
  • Weather updates when requested
  • Confirmation messages for business registrations

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on how often you text us questions. We only respond to your inquiries - we don't send promotional messages or regular updates unless specifically requested.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan. 854 COWS does not charge for our service, but your carrier may charge for SMS messages according to your plan.

How to Opt Out

To stop receiving messages:

  • Text STOP to 541-854-2697 at any time
  • You'll receive a confirmation that you've been unsubscribed
  • You can opt back in anytime by texting us a new question
