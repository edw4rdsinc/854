"Your Friendly Neighborhood Cow" - This perfectly captures the project's soul.

854 COWS is a love letter to Tillamook County. It's a simple tool built on the idea that technology should connect us to our community, not distract us from it. No apps, no logins, no barriers. Just text a question, and get a helpful answer from the friendliest cows on the Oregon Coast.

Our Core Values

  • Simplicity Over Complexity: If you can text, you can use 854 COWS. It's that easy.
  • Community Over Commerce: This is a free resource for locals, tourists, and businesses. We're here to connect people, not to build a sales funnel.
  • Authenticity Over Generic Tech: We're proudly Tillamook, not Silicon Valley. We celebrate local knowledge and local character.
  • Access Over Barriers: Works on any phone that can text. We aim to bridge the digital divide, not widen it.

The Vision

854 COWS is a model for community-centered technology. It asks: What if technology made places MORE local, not less? What if the barrier to community information was zero? We believe technology can serve communities without exploiting them, and we're starting right here in Tillamook.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's our simple promise:

  • We only store your phone number to communicate with you via SMS.
  • We do not share your phone number or conversation history with any third parties.
  • The content of your messages is used only to understand your request and provide a relevant answer.
  • We may analyze anonymized query data (e.g., "what are the tide times?") to improve the service, but this data is never linked to your personal phone number.
  • This is a community project. We are not in the business of selling data.
